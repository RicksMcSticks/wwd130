Overview

Purpose

"Adventure Awaits! A fun hub for rafting enthusiasts to plan trips, book adventures, explore top locations, track challenges, and connect with the rafting community. Share stories, get tips, and unlock badges as you conquer new rapids!"

Audience

Our target audience is adventurous individuals and families, aged 18-45, with moderate to high disposable income, seeking thrilling outdoor experiences. They want a seamless way to discover, plan, and book rafting trips, with tailored recommendations, real-time info, and a community feel. Their unmet need is a one-stop site for all things rafting. Most will access the site on mobile, so a mobile-friendly design is key.
